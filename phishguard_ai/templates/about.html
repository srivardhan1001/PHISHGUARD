About PhishGuard AI

PhishGuard AI is a hybrid phishing URL detector for academic and demo use. It combines a rule-based engine with external threat intelligence to estimate risk and provide actionable guidance.

  • Rule engine checks: HTTPS, IP-based URLs, length, subdomains, and keywords.
  • Threat intelligence via Google Safe Browsing.
  • Privacy-friendly: only URLs are evaluated; no credentials collected.
